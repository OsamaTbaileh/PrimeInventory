This app is a valuable tool for companies that operate across multiple locations. It simplifies the process of monitoring and tracking the movement of goods and products between different warehouses, providing an efficient way to manage and oversee these logistical operations.

  • Identification and Authentication: The app offers three distinct user roles for enhanced security:
    • Admin
    • Managers
    • Workers
  • Effortless Setup: Users can easily:
    • Create new locations
    • Define products
    • Record product movements between locations
  • Detailed Inventory Reports: Gain insights into your inventory with:
    • Location-specific inventory reports
    • Product location and statistical reports
  • Team Collaboration: Foster collaboration by creating and managing teams within the user base.
